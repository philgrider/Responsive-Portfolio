About Me

My name is Phillip Grider and I am the sole proprietor of Integrated Logic Solutions and my company is what's known in my industry (Audio Visual Control Integration) as a Crestron Services Provider. You may ask yourself, if not from my industry, what does that mean?

I provide a service to both manufactures and audio visual contracting firms as a certified Crestron programmer to configure, program and setup systems in a vast range of disciplins. I not only program a control system but also the Digital Signal Processors(DSP) to insure the room sounds as goods as it functions.

As a secondary role in my company Integrated Logic Solutions we provide installation Supervisor Services to IMAX Corporation. This job role provides an installation service to IMAX for new Cinema startups. We provide onsite supervision of the projection and sound systems an IMAX theater. Travel is definetly in the job description. The question is what continent have I not been on.
